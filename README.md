GreenTrace is a web-based platform designed to improve transparency and accountability in environmental procurement processes in Albania. It replaces traditional paper-based monitoring systems with a centralized digital solution, making it easier to track, verify, and report environmental compliance in construction and infrastructure projects.

The platform connects three main user groups:

Government Administrators who manage projects, set environmental standards, and monitor compliance.
Businesses (Contractors) who submit documentation, track milestones, and provide proof of environmental actions.
Journalists/Public Users who monitor projects, access data, and report environmental issues.
